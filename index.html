Four pillars, one convergence

The drivers of West Africa's economic transformation

Energy powers digital infrastructure. Digital transformation enables precision agriculture. Agribusiness creates the economic base for mineral value addition. Strategic minerals fuel the energy transition. This is the convergence thread at the heart of WAIIS.

I · Energy Trade & Industrialization

Cross-border power trade through WAPP, renewable corridors linking generation to industry, and mini-grids for productive use.

WAPPRenewablesIndustrialization

II · Strategic Minerals

Lithium, bauxite, iron ore, manganese, with beneficiation hubs, ASM formalization, and supply-chain sovereignty.

Value additionBeneficiationSovereignty

III · Agribusiness & Food Security

ECOWAS Rice Initiative, cold chains, agricultural corridors, school feeding programs, and clean-energy-powered supply chains.

Rice initiativeCold chainFood security

IV · Digital Transformation

Sovereign data centers, digital public infrastructure, Africa-designed AI governance, and the Digital Embassy model.

DPIAI governanceDigital embassy
